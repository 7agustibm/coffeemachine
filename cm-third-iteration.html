Third iteration

The machine has been upgraded and the drink maker is now able to make orange juice and to deliver extra drinks. You have to update your firmware to send the correct messages to the drink maker so that users can have orange juices or extra hot drinks

Use cases

  • I want to be able to buy a orange juice for 0,6 euro
  • I want to be able to have my coffee, chocolate or tea extra hot

Implementation details

Here are the new commands the drink maker implements in his protocol:

O:: ==> One orange juice
Ch:: ==> One extra hot coffee with no sugar
Hh:1:0 ==> One extra hot chocolate with one sugar and a stick
Th:2:0 ==> One extra hot chocolate with two sugar and a stick

Let us see if your implementation is flexible enough to welcome those changes with not too much hassle
